Meet Your Doctor

Dr. Ping He, L.Ac., Dipl. O.M.

Dr. Ping He received his medical training from both China and the United States — graduating from the prestigious Beijing University of Traditional Chinese Medicine and completing advanced studies at Florida College of Integrative Medicine.


Nationally certified as a "Diplomat in Oriental Medicine" and fully licensed by the state of North Carolina, Dr. He brings decades of clinical expertise to every patient. Before coming to the US, he owned and operated TCM clinics in China and served as a physician assistant in the surgical department of the Tenth People's Hospital, Sichuan Province.
