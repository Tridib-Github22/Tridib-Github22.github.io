Our Story

Dr. Ping He, L.Ac., Dipl. O.M.

Dr. Ping He is the founder and lead practitioner of Ping TCM Clinic, the Raleigh-Durham area's premier Traditional Chinese Medicine center. His dual education — grounded in both Eastern and Western medical traditions — gives him a uniquely comprehensive approach to patient care.


Dr. He earned his medical degree from the Beijing University of Traditional Chinese Medicine, one of China's most prestigious TCM institutions, and further completed his training at the Florida College of Integrative Medicine in the United States. He is nationally certified as a "Diplomat in Oriental Medicine" by the NCCAOM and fully licensed by the state of North Carolina.


Before moving to the United States, Dr. He owned and operated his own TCM clinic in China and served as a physician assistant in the surgical department of the Tenth People's Hospital in Sichuan Province, bringing rich clinical experience across both traditional and biomedical settings.
